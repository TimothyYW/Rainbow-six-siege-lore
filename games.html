Reactivation of rainbow

Rainbow was reactivated by a politician Aurelia Arnot in order to fight the threat of an organization known as the white mask who begin terrorising the world. There are five countries CTU join together to form rainbow, that is America (FBI SWAT), Germany (GSG-9), France (GIGN), Russia (Spetsnaz), and United Kingdom (SAS). From that point onward the member of rainbow begin to expand, but the lore does not expand.

  1. Year 1

    2. Year one is started in 2016 where Ubisoft starting to adding eight new operators.

    1. Season 1 Operation Black Ice

      2. Operation Black Ice was released on February 2, 2016, which bringing two new operators from JTF2 Canada and a new map, an attacker name Sébastien "Buck" Côté and a defender name Tina Lin "Frost" Tsang and a map called "Yacht".

    2. Season 2 Operation Dust Line

      3. Operation Dust Line was released on May 10, 2016 which bringing two new operators from Navy Seal America and a new map, an attacker name Craig "Blackbeard" Jenson and a defender name Meghan J. "Valkyrie" Castellano and a map called "Border"

    3. Season 3 Operation Skull Rain

      4. Operation Skull Rain was released on August 2, 2016 which bringing two new operators from BOPE Brazil and a new map, an attacker name Vicente "Capitão" Souza and a defender name Taina "Caveira" Pereira and a map called "Favela".

    4. Season 4 Operation Red Crow

      5. Operation Red Crow was released on November 17, 2016 which bring two new operators from SAT Japan, new map, and a map reworke an attacker name Yumiko Imagawa (Japanese: 今川 由美子[1], Imagawa Yumiko), codenamed Hibana and a defender name Masaru Enatsu (Japanese: 江夏 優[1], Enatsu Masaru), codenamed Echo, a map called "Skyscrapper" and a map rework "Barlett University".

  2. Year 2

    3. Year two is started in 2017, there are still no new update on the lore while six new operators joining team rainbow.

    1. Season 1 Operation Velvet Shell

      2. Operation Velvet Shell was released on February 7, 2017, which bringing two new operators from GEO Spain and a new map, an attacker name Ryad "Jackal" Ramírez Al-Hassar and a defender name Dr. Elena María Álvarez codenamed Mira, and a map called "Coastline".

    2. Season 2 Operation Health

      3. Operation Health was released on June 7, 2017 unlike seasons before this season does not releasing new operators and maps, this season focusing on fixing the game bugs, glitches, and issues. This include new matchmaking system and servers.

    3. Season 3 Operation Blood Orchid

      4. Operation Blood Orchid was released on August 29, 2017 which bringing not two but three new operators, where two are from SDU Hong Kong, one from GROM Poland, and a new map, an attacker name Siu Mei Lin (Chinese: 蕭美蓮[1]), codenamed Ying, and defenders name Liu Tze Long (Chinese: 廖子朗[1]), codenamed Lesion and Elżbieta "Ela" Bosak while the map called "Theme Park".

    4. Season 4 Operation White Noise

      5. Operation White Noise was released on November 20, 2017 as the final season of year two, similar to Operation Blood Orchid instead of release two new operators, Ubisoft release three new operators, two are from 707th SMB South Korea and also from GROM Poland, attackers name Grace Nam (Hangeul: 남은혜, Nam Eun Hye), codenamed Dokkaebi, Zofia Bosak which is Operator Ela older sister, and a defender name Masaru Enatsu (Japanese: 江夏 優[1], Enatsu Masaru), codenamed Echo, a map called "Tower"

  3. Year 3

    4. Year three is started in 2018, during the this time some piece of the lore has been update but only for the first season, and there are eight new operators joining team rainbow.

    1. Season 1 Operation Chimera

      2. Operation Chimera was released on March 6, 2018, which bringing two new operators from two different countries CTU that are already join rainbow those are France (GIGN), and Russia (Spetsnaz). Unlike other operators this unit specialize on fighting chemical, biological, radioactive, and nuclear threat, hence the codename CBRN threat unit. Also, unlike the previous operation this seasons introducing two new attackers names Olivier "Lion" Flament from GIGN France and Lera "Finka" Melnikova (Belarusian: Лера Мельнікава) from Belarus, and unfortunely there is no new map. But for the first time, Ubisoft has introduce a limited time event called Outbreak, this event does not related to lore.

    2. Season 2 Operation Para Bellum

      3. Operation Para Bellum was released on June 7, 2018 during this season bringing two new defenders from GIS Italy, new map, and for the release of new secondary gadget. Two new defenders name Aria "Alibi" de Luca from Libya, and Adriano "Maestro" Martello. New gadget is a bullet proof camera that can be placed on the wall, and the new map called "Villa".

    3. Season 3 Operation Grim Sky

      4. Operation Grim Sky was released on September 4, 2018, returning to releasing one attacker and defender, this operation is unique cause it does not release new map and creating a new CTU. starting with an attacker Erik "Maverick" Thorn from Delat force America and a first defender that is shield operator Morowa "Clash" Evans from Metropolian Police Service England, both operators recruited to the CTU that is belong to rainbow, The Urban Tactical Response Team, codenamed Grim Sky (GSUTR) which operate on dense urban environment. while there is no new map, but this operation reworking one of existing map called "Hereford Base".

    4. Season 4 Operation Wind Bastion

      5. Operation Wind Bastion was released on December 4, 2018 as the final season of year three, this season the lore has been update, although it is not shown in the trailer, but the white mask has been defeated by rainbow and their bank account has been cease. relseasing both attacker and defender from same CTU GIGR Morocco, and new map. Attacker name Sanaa El Maktoub (Arabic: سناء المكتوب), codename Nomad and defender name Jalal El Fassi (Arabic: جلال الفاسي), codenamed Kaid, and new map called "Fortress"

  4. Year 4

    5. New director

    After the defeat of white mask, Aurelia got an offer to become US secretary of state underneath of President baldwin, and rainbow has a new director under Dr. Harishva "Harry" Pandey.

    The program

    Harry becoming the new director of team rainbow, Harry wishes for the team to operate more efficient and be ready for every kind of situation. He decided to decentralized the team and create multiple facilities around the world, with the purpose for the operators to get along and train one another.

    1. Season 1 Operation Burnt Horizon

      2. Operation Burnt Horizon was released on March 6, 2019, as start of the year Burnt Horizon introducing two new operators from SASR Australia and a new map. Starting with an attacker named Tori Tallyo Fairous, codenamed Gridlock and a defender named Max Goose, codenamed Mozzie, and a new map called "Outback".

    2. Season 2 Operation Phantom Sight

      3. Operation Phantom Sight was released on June 11, 2019 during this season instead of two operators from same nation bringing two diffrent operators from seperate nation and two seperate CTU. An attacker named Karina Gaarddhøje, codenamed Nøkk from Jaeger Corps and a defender named Collinn McKinley, codenamed Warden from United State Secret Service. Unfortunely, there are no new map but only rework of exsiting map, Kafe Dostoyevsky Rework.

    3. Season 3 Operation Ember Rise

      4. Operation Ember Rise was released on September 11, 2019, similar like Phantom Sight this operation bringing two operatos from Latin America countries, an attacker from Peru named Azucena Rocío Quispe, codenamed Amaru part of APCA CTU and a defender from Mexico named César Ruiz Hernández, codenamed Goyo as part FES CTU. Just like Phantom Sight the reworking exsiting map which this time on the map called Kanal.

    4. Season 4 Operation Shifting Tide

      5. Operation Shifting Tide was released on December 3, 2019. Adding two new operators from two seperate countries, but part of fictional group called Night Haven which is a private military group, an attacker from India named Jaimini Kalimohan Shah (Hindi: जैमिनी कलिमोहन शाह), codenamed Kali and a defender from Kenya named Ngũgĩ Muchoki Furaha, codenamed Wamai, and reworking Theme Park as one of the exsiting map.

      During this operation there has been an update on the lore, Harry utilzing the money that was being cease from The White Mask account to hire the private military organization called the Night Haven, with the main reason to ensure that enemy organization did not hire them against rainbow and to keep a closer look on them,
