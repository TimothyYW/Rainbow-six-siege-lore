Reactivation of rainbow

Rainbow was reactivated by a politician Aurelia Arnot in order to fight the threat of an organization known as the white mask who begin terrorising the world. There are five countries CTU join together to form rainbow, that is America (FBI SWAT), Germany (GSG-9), France (GIGN), Russia (Spetsnaz), and United Kingdom (SAS). From that point onward the member of rainbow begin to expand, but the lore does not expand.

  1. Year 1

    Year one is started in 2016 where Ubisoft starting to adding eight new operators, it is can be found in Year 1

  2. Year 2

    Year two is started in 2017, there are still no new update on the lore while six new operators joining team rainbow. As for year two update can be seen in Year 2.

  3. Year 3

    Year three is started in 2018, during the this time some piece of the lore has been update but only for the first season, and there are eight new operators joining team rainbow. as can be seen in the Year 3.

  4. Year 4

    New director

    After the defeat of white mask, Aurelia got an offer to become US secretary of state underneath of President baldwin, and rainbow has a new director under Dr. Harishva "Harry" Pandey.

    The program

    Harry becoming the new director of team rainbow, Harry wishes for the team to operate more efficient and be ready for every kind of situation. He decided to decentralized the team and create multiple facilities around the world, with the purpose for the operators to get along and train one another.

    The follow-up where addition of an operators on Year 4 and a piece of the lore.

  5. Year 5

    Year five is started in 2020, there are a lot of new update on the lore begin to develop more and the gameplay improvement, with the number of operators that are being added reduce to six only.

    Siege Invitational 2020

    The siege invitational 2020 was an in-game event adding new cosmetic and limited time map, while in the lore is the first annual Tournament of Champion as public training exercises for the counter terrorism unit accross the globe and to test all operatos within rainbow what have they learn from the program.

    After the addition of night haven begin to bring some of their operators to team rainbow, but not all of them is night haven there are others, check it out in Year 5.

  6. Year 6

    Year six is started in 2021, the lore are continue to develop and also the gameplay, with number of operators that are being added reduce to four only.

    Siege Invitational 2021

    The siege invitational 2021 similar to the previous event with new maps and cosmetic. In the lore the tournament of champion divided into four team captaions Vicente "Capitão" Souza, Eliza "Ash" Cohen, Elena "Mira" Álvarez, and Chul Kyung Hwa (Hangeul: 화철경, Hwa Cheol Gyeong), codenamed Vigil. Harry have decided to added Jaimini Kalimohan Shah, codenamed Kali to Ash team with hope that it will improve their relation since both disliking each other.

    Ubisoft added a comic to fill the lore story outisde the gameplay and video and the comic book title six invitational, within the comic showing the Night Haven training and bonding with some member of team rainbow.

    The Playbook

    At the final match in the tournament Team Ash against Team Mira, Ash and Kali seems to still not getting along pretty well, operator Aruni as part of team Mira informing her team leader that Kali dislike taking order and it is consider as advantage team Mira has agains team Ash. During the match Ash and Dokkaebi was pinned down by Mira and her team, Kali took an opportunity to use Dokkaebi as bait and manage to took down two of the opponents and Ash took one down. Kali took down the last opponent and win the tournament, but wounded Ash right ear, despite the win Ash was already at her limit and confront Kali about if the scenario was real she would have use Ash and Dokkaebi as life bait and getting them killed, Kali respond without care and consider this just a game hence making Ash snap and punch her in the face. From this point onward many operators got divided and lore are getting intense.

    after the dispute that happen between Ash and Kali, rainbow decided to recruite more operators while night haven have other plan in place, which is reveal in Year 6.

  7. Year 7

    Year seven started in 2022, the lore of rainbow getting interesting where there will be diving deeper into rainbow and getting personal between rainbow operators.

    Siege Invitational 2022

    Although there are no trailer for SI 2022, but the team captains named are Eliza “Ash” Cohen, Jaimini Kalimohan “Kali” Shah, Taina “Caveira” Pereira, and Sanaa “Nomad” El Maktoub.

    Sister in Arms

    The night haven decided to leave team rainbow and bringing couple of operators with them, one of the opetaor is Elżbieta "Ela" Bosak.

    Night Haven situation

    Harry expected that night haven will leave team rainbow bringing other operators with them besides Ela there are also Jack "Pulse" Estrada, Monika "IQ" Weiss, James Porter codenamed "Smoke", and Lera "Finka" Melnikova. At the same time Harry has found four captains to form their own team, they are Gustave "Doc" Kateb, Jordan Trace codenamed "Thermite", Taina "Caveira" Pereira, and Yumiko Imagawa codenamed "Hibana".

    With night haven left team rainbow, causing the tension between them grow worse and it worsen as the season progressing, as can be seen in Year 7. Is night haven begin to show their true color or is there someone else pulling the string?

    Year eight started in 2023, the lore of rainbow continue revealing their squad with only two squad left need to be revealed and revelation of who responsible for all this skirmish and chaos that is being caused.

Harry in final act of insult to Deimos telling him that he find him boring before got shot and killed by Deimos. Deimos has stated that he is planning to kill all members of team rainbow.

    It is reveal that there is someone who pull a string on the shadown causing tension between rainbow and night haven, it is also reveal that rainbow no longer team but they are divided into four seperate teams, the rest of team will be revealed in Year 8.
