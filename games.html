Reactivation of rainbow

Rainbow was reactivated by a politician Aurelia Arnot in order to fight the threat of an organization known as the white mask who begin terrorising the world. There are four countries CTU join together to form rainbow, that is America (FBI SWAT), Germany (GSG-9), Russia (Spetznaz), and United Kingdom (SAS). From that point onward the member of rainbow begin to expand, but the lore does not expand.

  1. Year 1

    2. Year one is started in 2016 where Ubisoft starting to adding eight new operators.

    1. Season 1 Operation Black Ice

      2. Operation Black Ice was released on February 2, 2016, which bringing two new operators from JTF2 Canada and a new map, an attacker name Sébastien "Buck" Côté and a defender name Tina Lin "Frost" Tsang and a map called "Yacht".

    2. Season 2 Operation Dust Line

      3. Operation Dust Line was released on May 10, 2016 which bringing two new operators from Navy Seal America and a new map, an attacker name Craig "Blackbeard" Jenson and a defender name Meghan J. "Valkyrie" Castellano and a map called "Border"

    3. Season 3 Operation Skull Rain

      4. Operation Skull Rain was released on August 2, 2016 which bringing two new operators from BOPE Brazil and a new map, an attacker name Vicente "Capitão" Souza and a defender name Taina "Caveira" Pereira and a map called "Favela".

    4. Season 4 Operation Red Crow

      5. Operation Red Crow was released on November 17, 2016 which bring two new operators from SAT Japan, new map, and a map reworke an attacker name Yumiko Imagawa (Japanese: 今川 由美子[1], Imagawa Yumiko), codenamed Hibana and a defender name Masaru Enatsu (Japanese: 江夏 優[1], Enatsu Masaru), codenamed Echo, a map called "Skyscrapper" and a map rework "Barlett University".

  2. Year 2

    3. Year two is started in 2017, there are still no new update on the lore while six new operators joining team rainbow.

    1. Season 1 Operation Velvet Shell

      2. Operation Velvet Shell was released on February 7, 2017, which bringing two new operators from GEO Spain and a new map, an attacker name Ryad "Jackal" Ramírez Al-Hassar and a defender name Dr. Elena María Álvarez codenamed Mira, and a map called "Coastline".

    2. Season 2 Operation Health

      3. Operation Health was released on June 7, 2017 unlike seasons before this season does not releasing new operators and maps, this season focusing on fixing the game bugs, glitches, and issues. This include new matchmaking system and servers.

    3. Season 3 Operation Blood Orchid

      4. Operation Blood Orchid was released on August 29, 2017 which bringing not two but three new operators, where two are from SDU Hong Kong, one from GROM Poland, and a new map, an attacker name Siu Mei Lin (Chinese: 蕭美蓮[1]), codenamed Ying, and defenders name Liu Tze Long (Chinese: 廖子朗[1]), codenamed Lesion and Elżbieta "Ela" Bosak while the map called "Theme Park".

    4. Season 4 Operation White Noise

      5. Operation White Noise was released on November 20, 2017 as the final season of year two, similar to Operation Blood Orchid instead of release two new operators, Ubisoft release three new operators, two are from 707th SMB South Korea and also from GROM Poland, attackers name Grace Nam (Hangeul: 남은혜, Nam Eun Hye), codenamed Dokkaebi, Zofia Bosak which is Operator Ela older sister, and a defender name Masaru Enatsu (Japanese: 江夏 優[1], Enatsu Masaru), codenamed Echo, a map called "Tower"
