Reactivation of rainbow

Rainbow was reactivated by a politician Aurelia Arnot in order to fight the threat of an organization known as the white mask who begin terrorising the world. There are five countries CTU join together to form rainbow, that is America (FBI SWAT), Germany (GSG-9), France (GIGN), Russia (Spetsnaz), and United Kingdom (SAS). From that point onward the member of rainbow begin to expand, but the lore does not expand.

  1. Year 1

    Year one started in 2016 where Ubisoft began adding eight new operators.

    • Season 1 Operation Black Ice

      As the first season of rainbow, two new operators from JTF2 Canada and a new map were added. Starting with an attacker named Sébastien "Buck" Côté and a defender named Tina Lin "Frost" Tsang, and the new map named "Yacht".

    • Season 2 Operation Dust Line

      Operation Dust Line was released on May 10, 2016 which bringing two new operators from Navy Seal America and a new map, an attacker name Craig "Blackbeard" Jenson and a defender name Meghan J. "Valkyrie" Castellano and a map called "Border"

    • Season 3 Operation Skull Rain

      Operation Skull Rain was released on August 2, 2016, bringing two new operators from BOPE Brazil and a new map, an attacker name Vicente "Capitão" Souza and a defender name Taina "Caveira" Pereira and a map called "Favela".

    • Season 4 Operation Red Crow

      Operation Red Crow was released on November 17, 2016, bringing two new operators from SAT Japan, a new map, and a map rework: an attacker name Yumiko Imagawa (Japanese: 今川 由美子[1], Imagawa Yumiko), codenamed Hibana and a defender name Masaru Enatsu (Japanese: 江夏 優[1], Enatsu Masaru), codenamed Echo, a map called "Skyscrapper" and a map rework "Barlett University".

  2. Year 2

    Year two started in 2017. There were still no new updates on the lore while six new operators joined team rainbow.

    • Season 1 Operation Velvet Shell

      Operation Velvet Shell was released on February 7, 2017, bringing two new operators from GEO Spain and a new map, an attacker name Ryad "Jackal" Ramírez Al-Hassar and a defender name Dr. Elena María Álvarez codenamed Mira, and a map called "Coastline".

    • Season 2 Operation Health

      Operation Health was released on June 7, 2017 unlike seasons before this season does not releasing new operators and maps, this season focusing on fixing the game bugs, glitches, and issues. This include new matchmaking system and servers.

    • Season 3 Operation Blood Orchid

      Operation Blood Orchid was released on August 29, 2017, bringing not two but three new operators: two from SDU Hong Kong, one from GROM Poland, and a new map, an attacker name Siu Mei Lin (Chinese: 蕭美蓮[1]), codenamed Ying, and defenders name Liu Tze Long (Chinese: 廖子朗[1]), codenamed Lesion and Elżbieta "Ela" Bosak while the map called "Theme Park".

    • Season 4 Operation White Noise

      Operation White Noise was released on November 20, 2017 as the final season of year two. Similar to Operation Blood Orchid, Ubisoft released three new operators instead of two — two from 707th SMB South Korea and one from GROM Poland: attackers named Grace Nam (Hangeul: 남은혜, Nam Eun Hye), codenamed Dokkaebi, and Zofia Bosak, who is Operator Ela's older sister, and a new map called "Tower".

  3. Year 3

    Year three started in 2018. During this time some pieces of the lore were updated, but only for the first season, and eight new operators joined team rainbow.

    • Season 1 Operation Chimera

      Operation Chimera was released on March 6, 2018, bringing two new operators from two CTUs already in rainbow: France (GIGN) and Russia (Spetsnaz). Unlike other operators, this unit specializes in fighting chemical, biological, radioactive, and nuclear threats, hence the codename CBRN threat unit. This season introduced two new attackers: Olivier "Lion" Flament from GIGN France and Lera "Finka" Melnikova (Belarusian: Лера Мельнікава) from Belarus, and unfortunately there was no new map. For the first time, Ubisoft introduced a limited-time event called Outbreak, which is not related to the lore.

    • Season 2 Operation Para Bellum

      Operation Para Bellum was released on June 7, 2018, bringing two new defenders from GIS Italy, a new map, and a new secondary gadget. The two new defenders are Aria "Alibi" de Luca from Libya and Adriano "Maestro" Martello. The new gadget is a bulletproof camera that can be placed on a wall, and the new map is called "Villa".

    • Season 3 Operation Grim Sky

      Operation Grim Sky was released on September 4, 2018, returning to releasing one attacker and one defender. This operation is unique because it did not release a new map but instead created a new CTU. The new attacker is Erik "Maverick" Thorn from Delta Force America and the new defender is the shield operator Morowa "Clash" Evans from Metropolitan Police Service England. Both operators were recruited into the CTU belonging to rainbow, The Urban Tactical Response Team, codenamed Grim Sky (GSUTR), which operates in dense urban environments. There was no new map, but this operation reworked an existing map called "Hereford Base".

    • Season 4 Operation Wind Bastion

      Operation Wind Bastion was released on December 4, 2018 as the final season of year three. The lore was updated this season: although not shown in the trailer, the White Mask was defeated by rainbow and their bank accounts were seized. Both attacker and defender are from the same CTU, GIGR Morocco, alongside a new map. The attacker is Sanaa El Maktoub (Arabic: سناء المكتوب), codenamed Nomad, the defender is Jalal El Fassi (Arabic: جلال الفاسي), codenamed Kaid, and the new map is called "Fortress".

  4. Year 4

    New director

    After the defeat of the White Mask, Aurelia received an offer to become US Secretary of State under President Baldwin, and rainbow gained a new director in Dr. Harishva "Harry" Pandey.

    The program

    With Harry as the new director of team rainbow, he wished for the team to operate more efficiently and be ready for every kind of situation. He decided to decentralize the team and create multiple facilities around the world, with the purpose of allowing operators to get along with and train one another.

    • Season 1 Operation Burnt Horizon

      Operation Burnt Horizon was released on March 6, 2019, introducing two new operators from SASR Australia and a new map to start the year. The attacker is named Tori Tallyo Fairous, codenamed Gridlock, and the defender is named Max Goose, codenamed Mozzie, with a new map called "Outback".

    • Season 2 Operation Phantom Sight

      Operation Phantom Sight was released on June 11, 2019. This season brought two different operators from separate nations and two separate CTUs. The attacker is Karina Gaarddhøje, codenamed Nøkk from Jaeger Corps, and the defender is Collinn McKinley, codenamed Warden from the United States Secret Service. Unfortunately there was no new map, only a rework of an existing map: Kafe Dostoyevsky.

    • Season 3 Operation Ember Rise

      Operation Ember Rise was released on September 11, 2019. Similar to Phantom Sight, this operation brought two operators from Latin American countries: an attacker from Peru named Azucena Rocío Quispe, codenamed Amaru, part of the APCA CTU, and a defender from Mexico named César Ruiz Hernández, codenamed Goyo, part of the FES CTU. Like Phantom Sight, an existing map was reworked — this time the map called Kanal.

    • Season 4 Operation Shifting Tide

      Operation Shifting Tide was released on December 3, 2019, adding two new operators from two separate countries, both part of the fictional private military group Night Haven. The attacker from India is Jaimini Kalimohan Shah (Hindi: जैमिनी कलिमोहन शाह), codenamed Kali, and the defender from Kenya is Ngũgĩ Muchoki Furaha, codenamed Wamai. The existing map Theme Park was also reworked.

      During this operation the lore was updated: Harry utilized the money seized from The White Mask's accounts to hire the private military organization Night Haven, with the main goal of ensuring that enemy organizations could not hire them against rainbow and to keep a closer watch on them.

  5. Year 5

    Year five started in 2020. The lore continued to develop significantly alongside gameplay improvements, with the number of operators added reduced to six.

    Siege Invitational 2020

    The Siege Invitational 2020 was an in-game event adding new cosmetics and a limited-time map. In the lore it represented the first annual Tournament of Champions — a public training exercise for counter-terrorism units across the globe to test what all operators within rainbow had learned from the program.

    • Season 1 Operation Void Edge

      Operation Void Edge was released on March 10, 2020, bringing two new operators from two different countries and CTUs alongside a map rework: an attacker named Nienke "Iana" Meijer from REU Netherlands and a defender named Saif Al Hadid (Arabic: سيف الحديد), codenamed Oryx from GIGR (unofficially) Jordan, and a rework of the map "Oregon".

    • Season 2 Operation Steel Wave

      Operation Steel Wave was released on June 16, 2020. Similar to the previous operation, it brought an attacker from Night Haven named Håvard "Ace" Haugland from Norway and a defender named Thandiwe "Melusi" Ndlovu from ITF South Africa, along with a rework of the existing map "House". A new secondary gadget called the proximity alarm was also introduced, which can attach to all types of surfaces.

    • Season 3 Operation Shadow Legacy

      Operation Shadow Legacy was released on September 10, 2020. Unfortunately this operation marked the beginning of Ubisoft's decision to bring only one operator per season. The new operator is Samuel Leo "Sam" Fisher, codenamed Zero from United States ROS, and the map "Chalet" was reworked. There was also a rework for one of the Spetsnaz operators named Alexsandr Senaviev (Russian: Александр Сенавьев, tr. Aleksandr Senav'yev), codenamed Tachanka (Тачанка). Sam Fisher, codenamed Zero, is from another Ubisoft game called Splinter Cell. In Rainbow Six Siege, Sam was on the run and hiding within rainbow under the codename Zero, playing the role of instructor in the program.

    • Season 4 Operation Neon Dawn

      Operation Neon Dawn was released on December 1, 2020 as the final season of year five, bringing a defender from Night Haven named Apha "Aruni" Tawanroong (Thai: อาภา ตะวันรุ่ง) from Thailand, and a map rework for "Skyscraper". Aruni has a history with FBI SWAT operator Jordan "Thermite" Trace — they are old friends who were in an operation together in Bangkok that went wrong, causing Thermite to get burned and Aruni to lose her limbs.

  6. Year 6

    Year six started in 2021. The lore continued to develop alongside the gameplay, with the number of operators added reduced to four.

    Siege Invitational 2021

    The Siege Invitational 2021 was similar to the previous event with new maps and cosmetics. In the lore, the Tournament of Champions was divided into four teams, each led by a captain: Vicente "Capitão" Souza, Eliza "Ash" Cohen, Elena "Mira" Álvarez, and Chul Kyung Hwa (Hangeul: 화철경, Hwa Cheol Gyeong), codenamed Vigil. Harry decided to add Jaimini Kalimohan Shah, codenamed Kali, to Ash's team in the hope of improving their relationship, since both disliked each other.

    Ubisoft added a comic to fill the lore story outside the gameplay and video. The comic book is titled six invitational, within the comic showing the Night Haven training and bonding with some member of team rainbow.

    The Playbook

    At the final match, Team Ash faced Team Mira. Ash and Kali still were not getting along well. Operator Aruni, part of Team Mira, informed her team leader that Kali disliked taking orders, which was considered an advantage for Team Mira. During the match, Ash and Dokkaebi were pinned down by Mira and her team. Kali took the opportunity to use Dokkaebi as bait, taking down two opponents, while Ash took down one. Kali eliminated the last opponent and won the tournament, but wounded Ash's right ear. Despite the win, Ash had reached her limit and confronted Kali, arguing that in a real scenario Kali would have used her and Dokkaebi as live bait to get them killed. Kali responded without concern, treating it as just a game — causing Ash to snap and punch her in the face. From this point, many operators became divided and the lore grew more intense.

    • Season 1 Crimson Heist

      Crimson Heist was released on March 16, 2021. For the first time, this season dropped the word "Operation" from its name and brought only one new operator: an attacker from Argentina named Santiago "Flores" Lucero, who is not part of any CTU. A new attacker secondary gadget — a pocket grenade launcher to destroy defender gadgets and breach barricades (Gonne-6) — was also added, along with a rework of the existing map "Border". Flores was a master thief who was offered asylum by Ash back in 2016 because he was hunted by a crime lord for stealing their money. In early 2021, Ash sent Miles "Castle" Campbell and César "Goyo" Hernández to recruit Flores with a task for him. This story is told in the comic titled Crimson Heist.

    • Season 2 North Star

      North Star was released on June 14, 2021, bringing a defender from STAR-NET Aviation Canada named Mina "Thunderbird" Sky, and a rework of the map "Favela". During her recruitment she was attacked, as shown in the comic titled North Star.

    • Season 3 Crystal Guard

      Crystal Guard was released on September 7, 2021, bringing an attacker from Night Haven named Anja Katarina "Osa" Janković from Croatia, and small reworks on existing maps such as Coastline, Clubhouse, and Bank. Osa is revealed to be the head of Quantum Concept Robotics, in charge of developing Night Haven's tech. It is also revealed that the reason Night Haven operators joined rainbow was so that Kali could recruit rainbow operators to Night Haven by inviting them to Night Haven's base to try out their tech. A comic about Osa was released by Ubisoft titled Crystal Guard.

    • Season 4 High Calibre

      High Calibre was released on November 30, 2021, as the final season of Year 6, releasing a defender from Garda ERU Ireland named Brianna "Thorn" Skehan, and a rework of the map "Outback". Thorn is an old friend of Seamus "Sledge" Cowden and joined Team Caveira for SI 2022. A comic showing Caveira and Thorn spending time together was released, titled High Calibre.

  7. Year 7

    Year seven started in 2022. The lore of rainbow became increasingly interesting, diving deeper into rainbow's structure and the personal relationships between operators.

    Siege Invitational 2022

    Although there was no trailer for SI 2022, the team captains were Eliza “Ash” Cohen, Jaimini Kalimohan “Kali” Shah, Taina “Caveira” Pereira, and Sanaa “Nomad” El Maktoub.

    Sister in Arms

    Night Haven decided to leave team rainbow, bringing a couple of operators with them. One of those operators is Elżbieta "Ela" Bosak.

    Night Haven situation

    Harry expected that Night Haven would leave team rainbow and bring other operators with them. Besides Ela, there were also Jack "Pulse" Estrada, Monika "IQ" Weiss, James Porter codenamed "Smoke", and Lera "Finka" Melnikova. At the same time, Harry appointed four captains to form their own teams: Gustave "Doc" Kateb, Jordan Trace codenamed "Thermite", Taina "Caveira" Pereira, and Yumiko Imagawa codenamed "Hibana".

    • Season 1 Demon Veil

      Demon Veil was released on March 16, 2022, bringing a defender named Kana "Azami" Fujiwara (Japanese: 藤原加奈, Fujiwara Kana) from Japan, the first new map named "Emerald Plains", and a new game mode, Team Deathmatch (TDM). Azami was a police officer in Japan who was fired and began working as a bodyguard, hired by Masayuki Yahata. Azami and Yahata grew to be good friends. During a stroll through a street market in Japan they were ambushed by assassins. Azami fought back, but it was not enough and it cost Yahata his life. Enraged and seeking to avenge him, Azami contacted her old friend Yumiko "Hibana" Imagawa and showed her a bullet casing with the QCR brand on it. The investigation of Yahata's death continues in the comic titled Demon Veil. Within the comic it is revealed how some operators reacted to members of team rainbow joining Night Haven — including Mike "Thatcher" Baker's opinion on Smoke joining Night Haven.

    • Season 2 Operation Vector Glare

      Operation Vector Glare was released on June 14, 2022, returning "Operation" to the season's name. This operation released an attacker from SFG Belgium named Néon Ngoma Mutombo, codenamed Sens, and a new TDM map called "Close Quarter". This season revealed the team led by Doc, codenamed Wolf Guard, with Sens joining them. They were pursuing an architect belonging to Night Haven in order to infiltrate their base and uncover Night Haven's involvement in Yahata's death. After the rescue, the next comic titled Vector Glare showcases the effort to infiltrate Night Haven.

    • Season 3 Operation Brutal Swarm

      Operation Brutal Swarm was released on September 6, 2022, bringing an attacker from Night Haven named Charlie Tho “Grim” Keng Boon from Singapore, adding a new map from the previous SI called “Stadium Bravo”, and small additions to the ranked playlist. Night Haven discovered that QCR was being infiltrated by an unknown enemy and sent Grim and other operators including James “Smoke” Porter. Grim decided to go in alone and got into close combat with Karina “Nøkk” Gaarddhøje. Grim had the upper hand, but Nøkk had an escape plan — detonating a charge on the wall and escaping with a list of QCR shipments. Afterward, Kali discovered that rainbow had infiltrated QCR and assigned Grim to find the operator who escaped, as shown in the comic titled Brutal Swarm.

    • Season 4 Operation Solar Raid

      Operation Solar Raid was released on December 6, 2022 as the final season of Year 7, releasing a defender from AFEAU Colombia named Ana "Solis" Valentina Díaz, and a new map called "Nighthaven Lab". This season revealed the next squad led by Caveira, codenamed "Ghosteye". Solis had a hard time in her training under Ghosteye, as shown in the comic titled Solar Raid. Later it was discovered that Night Haven was not responsible for Yahata's death and that their merchandise had been stolen. Ghosteye confronted Kali and made clear that Night Haven should stay out.

  8. Year 8

    Year eight started in 2023. The lore of rainbow continued revealing its squads, with only two squads left to be introduced, and the revelation of who was responsible for all the skirmishes and chaos that had been caused.

    Siege Invitational 2023

    Although there was no trailer for SI 2023, it was revealed that the person responsible for all the trouble rainbow suffered goes by the name "Deimos". He showed Harry a live feed from Thermite's point of view — retrieving Night Haven's stolen merchandise, assisted by Caveira, Sledge, Zofia, and Ash. Unfortunately, it was a trap set up by Deimos. He claimed that Harry had disgraced his unit, to which Harry replied that it was their unit and that he was only there to support and guide them. In a final act of defiance, Harry told Deimos he found him boring — before being shot and killed by Deimos. Deimos declared that he planned to kill all members of team rainbow.

    • Season 1 Operation Commanding Force

      Operation Commanding Force was released on March 7, 2023, bringing an attacker from COT Brazil named Nayara "Brava" Cardoso alongside gameplay improvements. This season revealed another squad named "ViperStrike", led by Hibana. In the lore, Brava is Capitão's cousin — both joined ViperStrike. Together with Hibana they hunted down Deimos, who was on a plane. Hibana, Capitão, and Brava almost had him, but he managed to escape. Despite their failure, they managed to hurt him financially. Afterward, Brava visited the hospital in Hereford where it was revealed that Thermite and other operators set up by Deimos were injured and Ash was in a coma. The story continues in the comic titled Commanding Force. At the end of the comic, Brava mentions someone who would be revealed in the next season.

    • Season 2 Operation Dread Factor

      Operation Dread Factor was released on May 30, 2023. It came as no surprise that the last squad was led by Thermite, named "Red Hammer", with a new defender from Sweden named Emil "Fenrir" Svensson. Fenrir used to work for Deimos — not by choice, as he had little option. He was an outcast in his family and was discharged from the military because of his desire to develop technology to alter soldiers' emotions. Deimos, interested in Fenrir's work, decided to fund him and provide facilities, but the two had different intentions: one wished to make the world a better place while the other wanted to unleash chaos upon it. Fenrir found out the hard way by witnessing Deimos use his invention on his colleagues and watching them perish. After seeing Deimos's deception, Fenrir fled and joined Red Hammer. Fenrir and Red Hammer's efforts to intercept Deimos's plan are told in the comic titled Dread Factor.

    • Season 3 Operation Heavy Mettle

      Operation Heavy Mettle was released on August 29, 2023, introducing a new attacker named Britta "Ram" Nilsson from Sweden's SSG (Särskilda Skyddsgruppen). Ram's unique gadget is the BU-GI Auto Breacher — a remote-controlled device that crawls along the floor and breaches reinforced walls and barricades, giving attackers a new way to create openings without exposing themselves. There was no new map this season, but several gameplay improvements were introduced. More lore details coming soon.
