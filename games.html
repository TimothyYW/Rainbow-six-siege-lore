Reactivation of rainbow

Rainbow was reactivated by a politician Aurelia Arnot in order to fight the threat of an organization known as the white mask who begin terrorising the world. There are five countries CTU join together to form rainbow, that is America (FBI SWAT), Germany (GSG-9), France (GIGN), Russia (Spetsnaz), and United Kingdom (SAS). From that point onward the member of rainbow begin to expand, but the lore does not expand.

    5. New director

    After the defeat of white mask, Aurelia got an offer to become US secretary of state underneath of President baldwin, and rainbow has a new director under Dr. Harishva "Harry" Pandey.

    The program

    Harry becoming the new director of team rainbow, Harry wishes for the team to operate more efficient and be ready for every kind of situation. He decided to decentralized the team and create multiple facilities around the world, with the purpose for the operators to get along and train one another.

    The follow-up where addition of an operators on Year 4 and a piece of the lore.

Kali took down the last opponent and win the tournament, but wounded Ash right ear, despite the win Ash was already at her limit and confront Kali about if the scenario was real she would have use Ash and Dokkaebi as life bait and getting them killed, Kali respond without care and consider this just a game hence making Ash snap and punch her in the face.
