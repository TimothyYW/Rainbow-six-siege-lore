  1. Season 1 Operation Chimera

    Operation Chimera was released on March 6, 2018, which bringing two new operators from two different countries CTU that are already join rainbow those are France (GIGN), and Russia (Spetsnaz). Unlike other operators this unit specialize on fighting chemical, biological, radioactive, and nuclear threat, hence the codename CBRN threat unit. Also, unlike the previous operation this seasons introducing two new attackers names Olivier "Lion" Flament from GIGN France and Lera "Finka" Melnikova (Belarusian: Лера Мельнікава) from Belarus, and unfortunely there is no new map. But for the first time, Ubisoft has introduce a limited time event called Outbreak, this event does not related to lore.

  2. Season 2 Operation Para Bellum

    Operation Para Bellum was released on June 7, 2018 during this season bringing two new defenders from GIS Italy, new map, and for the release of new secondary gadget. Two new defenders name Aria "Alibi" de Luca from Libya, and Adriano "Maestro" Martello. New gadget is a bullet proof camera that can be placed on the wall, and the new map called "Villa".

  3. Season 3 Operation Grim Sky

    Operation Grim Sky was released on September 4, 2018, returning to releasing one attacker and defender, this operation is unique cause it does not release new map and creating a new CTU. starting with an attacker Erik "Maverick" Thorn from Delat force America and a first defender that is shield operator Morowa "Clash" Evans from Metropolian Police Service England, both operators recruited to the CTU that is belong to rainbow, The Urban Tactical Response Team, codenamed Grim Sky (GSUTR) which operate on dense urban environment. while there is no new map, but this operation reworking one of existing map called "Hereford Base".

  4. Season 4 Operation Wind Bastion

    Operation Wind Bastion was released on December 4, 2018 as the final season of year three, this season the lore has been update, although it is not shown in the trailer, but the white mask has been defeated by rainbow and their bank account has been cease. relseasing both attacker and defender from same CTU GIGR Morocco, and new map. Attacker name Sanaa El Maktoub (Arabic: سناء المكتوب), codename Nomad and defender name Jalal El Fassi (Arabic: جلال الفاسي), codenamed Kaid, and new map called "Fortress"
