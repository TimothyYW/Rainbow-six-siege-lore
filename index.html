What is Rainbow Six Siege?

Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical and elite first person shooter was developed by Ubisoft Montreal and was based on the novel on 3rd August 1998 by Tom clancy title Rainbow Six, The game was release in 1 December 2015, where two teams consist of five members compete against each other. One team play role as attacker and the opposite side as defender with three different modes such as Bomb, Hostage, and Secure Area. It is available on PC via Uplay or Steam, and on Console such as Xbox and Playstation. Till this day, Rainbow Six Siege has been developed after eight years and still develop in gameplay and lore. and growing number of players, If you wish to explore more? Please click here!
