    Operation Burnt Horizon was released on March 6, 2019, as start of the year Burnt Horizon introducing two new operators from SASR Australia and a new map. Starting with an attacker named Tori Tallyo Fairous, codenamed Gridlock and a defender named Max Goose, codenamed Mozzie, and a new map called "Outback".
