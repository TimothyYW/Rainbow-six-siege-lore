  2. Season 2 Operation Steel Wave

    3. Operation Steel Wave was released on June 16, 2020, similar with previous operation bringing an attacker from Night Haven named Håvard "Ace" Haugland from Norway and a defender named Thandiwe "Melusi" Ndlovu from ITF South Africa, and reworking exisiting map called "House". There is a new secondary gadget called proximity alarm which can attach to all type of surface.

  3. Season 3 Operation Shadow Legacy

    4. Operation Shadow Legacy was released on September 10, 2020, Unfortunely this operation the beginning of Ubisoft decision to bring only one operator, the new operator named Samuel Leo "Sam" Fisher, codenamed Zero from United State ROS, and rework for map called "Chalet". But there is another rework for one of Spetsnaz operator named Alexsandr Senaviev (Russian: Александр Сенавьев, tr. Aleksandr Senav'yev), codenamed Tachanka (Тачанка). Sam Fisher or codenamed Zero is from another game of Ubisoft called splinter cell, in rainbow six siege Sam was on the run and hiding in rainbow under the codenamed Zero, and play role as instructor in the program.

  4. Season 4 Operation Neon Dawn

    5. Operation Neon Dawn was released on December 1, 2020 as the final season of year five, by bringing an defender from Night Haven named Apha "Aruni" Tawanroong (Thai: อาภา ตะวันรุ่ง) from Thailand, and map rework for "Skyscrapper". Aruni has a history with one of FBI SWAT operator named Jordan "Thermite" Trace, they are old friend and in an operation together in Bangkok and it went south causing Thermite got burned and Aruni lost her limps.
