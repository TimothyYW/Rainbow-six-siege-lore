  1. Season 1 Demon veil

    Demon veil was releasedMarch 16, 2021, bringing a defender named Kana "Azami" Fujiwara (Japanese: 藤原加奈, Fujiwara Kana) from Japan for the first time adding a new map named "Emerald Plain", and new game mode Team Death Match (TDM). Azami was a police officer in Japan who got fired and working as a bodyguard and was hired by Masayuki Yahata. Azami and Yahata grow to be a good friend. During their stroll through street market in Japan they were ambush by assassins Azami fought back, sadly it was not good enough and it cost Yahata`s life. Enraged and wanted to avenge Azami contacted her old friend Yumiko "hibana" Imagawa and showed her a bullet case with QCR brand on it. The investigation of Yahata`s death continue with the comic titled Demon Veil. Within the comic it is reveal how some operators react to some members of team rainbow joining night have such as, Mike "Thatcher" Baker opinion on Smoke joining night haven.

  2. Season 2 Operation Vector Glare

    Operation Vector Glare was released on June 14, 2022, returning to operation to the name of the season, this operation releasing an attacker from SFG Belgium named Néon Ngoma Mutombo, codenamed Sens, and new map for TDM "Close Quarter". This season reveal the team that was lead by Doc where Sens joining in codenamed Wolf Guard, and currently they are going after the architect that belong to the night haven to infiltrate their base to find out about night haven involvement with Yahata`s death. After the rescue, it is reveal in the next comic titled Vector Glare show casing the effort to infiltrate night haven.

  3. Season 3 Operation Brutal Swarm

    Operation Brutal Swarm was released on September 6, 2022, an attacker from Night Haven named Charlie Tho “Grim” Keng Boon from Croatia, adding new map from previous SI called "Stadium Bravo", and small addition to ranked playlist. Night haven discover that QCR is being infilitrated by unknown enemy and they send Grim and other operators including James "Smoke" Porter, Grim decided to go in alone and getting into close combat with Karina "Nøkk" Gaarddhøje. Grim got the upper hand, but Nøkk got an escape plan in place by detonating a charge on the wall and escape with the list of QCR shipment. Afterward, Kali discover that rainbow infiltrate the QCR and assign Grim to find who was the operator that escape, as can be seen in the comic titled Brutal Swarm.

  4. Season 4 Operation Solar Raid

    Operation solar raid was released on December 6th, 2022. as final season for Year 7 releasing a defender from AFEAU Colombia named Ana "Solis" Valentina Díaz, and a new map called "Nighthave Lab". During this season it is reveal the next squad that is lead by Caveira codenamed "Ghosteye" and Solis having a hard time in her training with Ghosteye as can be seen in comic titled Solar Raid. Later on, it is discover that night haven does not responsible for Yahata`s death and their merchendise has been stolen, Ghosteye confront Kali and ensuring that night haven staying out.
