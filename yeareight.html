  2. Season 2 Operation Dread Factor

    Operation Dread Factor was released on May 30th, 2023, it is not a surprised that the last squad being led by Thermite and the squad named "Red Hammer", releasing a defender from Sweden Emil "Fenrir" Svensson. Fenrir used to work for Deimos, not by choice since he does not have much choice, he was an outcast to his family and he got discharge from military because of his wishes to develop a technology to alter soldier emotion. Deimos interested on Fenrir work decided to funding and providing him with facility, but Fenrir and Deimos has different intention with one wishes to make a world a better place while the other just wanna unleashed chaos on the world. Fenrir found out the hard way by seeing Deimos using his invention on his colleague and witnessed their demise. After seeing Deimos deception, Fenrir fled and joining Red Hammer. Fenrir and Red Hammer trying to intercept Deimos plan in comic titled Dread Factor.
